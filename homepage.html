This Week's Film
2024
Sunsong Cinema

We select films that matter. They change lives. They connect. They help you reconnect with those around you, and with yourself. They nourish the love for the world, and the love for everything the world is not yet. They blissfully do nothing at all — pure delight and pleasure. They are political. They cuddle. They embrace. They make love. They commemorate genocides and also already the first smile of your great-great-grandchild, years from now. They polarize. They unite. They destabilize. They stabilize. A river is nothing compared to them. They are heartbreaking and complete as a lotus flower. They are unforgettable, because they help us forget and forgive. They enrage and set us on fire. They help us begin anew, because they help us leave things behind. They are historical, because they are signs of our times. They do what movies should do: move.

Sunsong Cinema is your carefully curated selection. Out of ten thousand new films every year, we select those that have proven to be extraordinarily beneficial. We simply want to pass them on.
